## 1.0.0

Initial release to public.

1. New trigger! triggers/document_created — fires the moment a document is sent for signature.
2. New trigger! triggers/signer_signed — fires each time an individual signer signs.
3. New trigger! triggers/document_completed — fires once every signer has signed.
4. New action! creates/send_document_from_template — sends a saved Docracy template out for
   signature, picking signers by name/email.
